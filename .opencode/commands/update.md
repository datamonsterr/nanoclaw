Run the NanoClaw update skill to bring upstream updates into a customized install. This skill:

1. Fetches upstream changes from the qwibitai/nanoclaw remote
2. Shows a preview of available updates
3. Lets you selectively cherry-pick commits
4. Handles merge conflicts gracefully
5. Preserves your local customizations

Use this when you want to stay up-to-date with NanoClaw development while keeping your custom channels and configurations.
